About Us

Greetings! We are Nook's Cranny, we provide goods such as Furniture, Wall Papers, Hats, and much much more. Established in the early 1900s, our founder Tom Nook started this business with the intent to serve his community the finest home goods in America. Nook started his business in his parents garage at the humble age of thirty-two, as the years went on Nook's Cranny became a household name and hopes to be for the years to come.
